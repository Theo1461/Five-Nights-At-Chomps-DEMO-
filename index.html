Game Information:

This is Five Nights At Chomp's a game based on FNAF.

Chomps is the candy corn robot on stage he will leave the stage and go to the hallway, to prevent him from going into the office turn OFF the power from the generator room, he will go away soon after.

Mouse Pointer is the gray mouse pointer seen on stage he will leave the stage and enter the hallway, he is quite the trickster and instead of jumpscaring you he will just disable your camera and door, to prevent this toggle the door. (You can only use the door if the power is on.)

Clawed is the glove seen on stage and is the most aggressive and will leave the stage and head to the vent in which you will have to open the vent camera to stop him from entering the office.

Remember there are 2 old robots inside the backroom in which you should not use or touch, if one of them exits (Unlikely) just use the same techniques for them as the others depending on where they are, remember the backroom is behind the door inside of storage and is always off camera.
